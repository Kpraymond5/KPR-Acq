Acquiring small businesses to build big businesses

Kevin Raymond

I am the founder of KPR Acquisitions. I started my career on Wall Street underwriting multi-billion dollar Mergers and Acquisitions. But I was too entrepreneurial to be trapped in the corporate world. I was involved in several startups then soon realized the appeal of buying an existing, cash flowing business - it's really the best of both worlds. There is obviously risk with any investment, but with an existing business you benefit from the existing cash flow (like a corporate job) and have the potential for serious monetary upside (like a startup).

I am based in New York City and am a graduate of Bowdoin College.

I look forward to discussing how we can turn a small business into a large profitable company.
