Who We Work With

We partner with individuals or groups looking to acquire an existing business - both experienced and first time buyers

Active vs. Passive Owners

Currently we are only partnering with entrepreneurs that plan to be involved in the day-to-day of the business operations

What stage in the process should you contact us

We are contacted at any pre-acquisition phase - Whether you are just exploring the idea of potentially buying a business or you have an LOI in place

Minimum Capital Requirement

11% of the purchase price in a levered deal, 51% of the purchase price in an unlevered deal

Partnership Structure

-KPR invests in a minority equity stake in existing businesses, alongside the buyer(s)

-We will own between 5% and 20% of a business
